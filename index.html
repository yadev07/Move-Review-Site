Inception (2010)
Reviewed by: Anjali Sharma
Rating:

Inception (2010) is one of those movies that really messes with your head — in a good way. It’s got dreams inside dreams, cool action, and a story that keeps you thinking. Leo totally nailed it, and the music just gives you chills. Definitely one of those films you can watch more than once and still catch something new.

RRR (2022)
Reviewed by: rajeev Mishra
Rating:

RRR is wild, loud, and a total blast. The action’s crazy, the emotions hit hard, and Ram Charan and Jr. NTR are just awesome. It’s over-the-top in the best way, and somehow it all works perfectly.

Interstellar (2014)
Reviewed by: Shruti Verma
Rating:

Interstellar is a beautiful mix of science, emotion, and mind-blowing visuals. It takes you across galaxies but keeps its heart rooted in love and human connection. Matthew McConaughey delivers big time, and the soundtrack is just otherworldly. It’s deep, emotional, and totally unforgettable.
